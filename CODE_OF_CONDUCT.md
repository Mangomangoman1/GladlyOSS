# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Gladly a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, identity, or perspective.

## Our standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Showing empathy and respect.
- Giving constructive, specific feedback.
- Welcoming questions from people who are still learning.
- Accepting responsibility and apologizing when needed.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, code, issues, and other contributions that violate these standards. They may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation when needed to protect the community.

For private conduct reports, contact a maintainer through an appropriate private GitHub channel. Every report will be reviewed with care.
